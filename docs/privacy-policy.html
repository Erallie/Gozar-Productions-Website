privacy policy

Introduction

At Gozar Productions, accessible from gozarproductions.com and all its subdomains, we value the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

This Privacy Policy applies to gozarproductions.com and all of its subdomains. However, it does not apply to any code-based projects that Gozar Productions provides, even if they are hosted on our subdomains. Those projects may have their own separate privacy policies.

If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to contact us at contact@gozarproductions.com.

Information We Collect

We do not actively collect personal information from visitors. However, the following data may be processed:

Cookies (Stored on Your Device)

We use cookies solely for user preferences:

  • Light/Dark Mode Preference – To remember your preferred theme.
  • Volume Preference – To save your audio settings for a better user experience.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Data Collection

While we do not collect personal data ourselves, certain third-party services may process data when you interact with them:

  • Cloudflare – Provides security and performance enhancements. Cloudflare may collect IP addresses and other technical data. (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages – Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect visitor data such as IP addresses and browser details. (GitHub Privacy Policy)
  • PayPal – If you choose to donate, PayPal may collect personal and financial information. (PayPal Privacy Policy)

External Links & Embedded Content

Our website contains links to third-party platforms. These services are not operated by us and may collect data according to their own privacy policies. These include, but are not limited to:

  • YouTube, GitHub, Discord (linked from gozarproductions.com)
  • Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, YouTube Music, Pandora, Amazon Music (linked from music.gozarproductions.com)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these platforms before interacting with them.

How We Use Your Information

We only use stored cookies to enhance your browsing experience. We do not collect, store, or share personal data ourselves.

Data Sharing & Security

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not share any with third parties. Any data collected by external platforms is managed under their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights & Choices

As we do not process personal data, there are no user accounts or stored information to access, modify, or delete. However, you may:

  • Manage Cookies – Adjust your browser settings to control or disable cookies.
  • Review Third-Party Policies – If you interact with PayPal, Cloudflare, GitHub Pages, or any linked services, refer to their privacy policies for details on your rights.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
